Most Popular

On-Grid Solar

Best suited for: Homes, offices, institutions, and industries with reliable grid power.

  • Maximum savings
  • No backup required
  • Lowest upfront cost
  • Net metering benefits
FACT: A well-sized on-grid system can reduce electricity bills by 70-90%.
Full Independence

Off-Grid Solar

Best suited for: Remote locations, campuses, and areas with unreliable or no grid supply.

  • Total energy independence
  • Battery-powered backup
  • Works without grid
  • Ideal for remote areas
FACT: Off-grid solar works completely without the main electricity grid.
Best of Both

Hybrid Solar

Best suited for: Users seeking savings, backup power, and grid interaction.

  • Backup during grid failures
  • Lower electricity bills
  • Grid export benefits
  • Homes, hospitals, data centers
FACT: Hybrid systems ensure power continuity while allowing grid export earnings.
